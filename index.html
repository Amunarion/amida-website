WHAT IS PROGRESSIVE DEATHCORE?

Progressive Deathcore represents a fusion of different styles from the metal genre, mainly Progressive Metal and Deathcore. The core elements are guitar riffs around blast beats as well as complex rhythm structures acompanied by guttural vocals. The guitars are usually tuned down.
